Age Verification

Proves a holder's age via boolean age-over predicates and/or a selectively disclosable birthdate. Designed so a holder can present only a predicate (e.g. age_over_18) without revealing birthdate.

Claims

Claim Type Required Selectively disclosable Notes
birthdate string <date> No always Full date of birth (ISO 8601 full-date). PRIVACY: must never be mandatory-disclosed when an age-over predicate suffices.
age_over_13 boolean No allowed Predicate: holder is at least 13 years old.
age_over_18 boolean No allowed Predicate: holder is at least 18 years old.
age_over_21 boolean No allowed Predicate: holder is at least 21 years old.

Auto-derived from schema.json + SD-JWT VC type metadata. always = issuer MUST make selectively disclosable; allowed = MAY; — = not individually disclosable.
